## Vision

TeachWhere is a decision-support platform helping teachers answer
**'Where should I teach?'** rather than simply listing jobs.

## Core Principle

Find the punter a good job in the right place as quickly and painlessly
as possible.

Every feature should help someone decide whether to click Apply.

## Product Position

-   Live vacancies
-   Schools database
-   Location intelligence
-   Incentives
-   Community information
-   Mapping
-   Relocation research

Schools are the permanent asset; vacancies are transient.

## Data Philosophy

Use the Department as the single source of truth for job details. Store
enough to search/filter (IDs, title, dates, salary, apply URL, parsed
role, parsed subject, HTML description) but avoid duplicating selection
criteria and application instructions.

## Vacancy Sources

1.  JobFeed
2.  I Work For NSW JSON
3.  Priority Recruitment emails
4.  Teacher Recruitment emails

## Permanent Pages

### Schools

Permanent school profiles with current vacancies. \### Locations
Permanent town hubs containing schools, live jobs, housing, crime,
climate, videos, lifestyle and transport.

## Map

The map is the product. Coloured markers, clustering, sidebar, filters
and eventually a 'Surprise Me' feature.

## Future Layers

Crime, housing, rent, weather, climate, population, hospitals, airports,
shopping, Department videos, travel times, teacher housing, vacancy
history, demand by subject and relocation guides.

## SEO

Real-time map; expired jobs disappear. SEO is built on permanent School
and Location pages whose job sections update dynamically.

## Branding

Working name: **TeachWhere**.
